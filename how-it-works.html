How Nortava Works

Simple steps to find or list your property

For Tenants

Finding Your New Home

1

Browse Listings

Search through available properties in Norton. Use filters to narrow down by price, rooms, and location. All browsing is completely free.

2

Save Favorites

Create a free account and save properties you're interested in. Compare them later and keep track of your search.

3

Unlock Contact

When you find the perfect place, pay just $2.50 to unlock the landlord's contact details. Use EcoCash, OneMoney, Mukuru, or InnBucks.

4

Connect Directly

Call or WhatsApp the landlord directly. Schedule viewings and negotiate terms on your own timeline.

For Landlords

Listing Your Property

1

Create Account

Register as a landlord for free. Provide basic information to help tenants trust your listings.

2

Add Your Property

Fill in property details - title, description, price, location. Upload up to 3 photos to showcase your property.

3

Go Live Instantly

Your listing is published immediately. Tenants can start viewing it right away - no approval delays.

4

Receive Inquiries

When tenants unlock your contact, they'll reach out directly. Get real-time notifications when someone shows interest.

FAQs

Common Questions

Why do I need to pay to unlock contacts?

The small fee helps ensure serious inquiries only. It reduces spam for landlords and keeps the platform sustainable. You pay once and have permanent access to that contact.

Is listing really free for landlords?

Yes! There are no listing fees, subscription costs, or hidden charges for landlords. You only benefit when tenants show real interest by unlocking your contact.

How do I know landlords are legitimate?

We verify landlords through our verification process. Look for the verified badge. Always follow our safety tips and view properties before paying any deposits.

What payment methods are accepted?

We accept EcoCash, OneMoney, Mukuru, and InnBucks - all the mobile payment methods you're familiar with in Zimbabwe.
