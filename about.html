About Nortava

The local-first real estate platform for Norton, Zimbabwe

Our Mission

Nortava was born from a simple idea: make finding and listing properties in Norton as easy as possible. We understand the challenges of house hunting in our community - the endless calls, unreliable listings, and scattered information. That's why we built a platform that brings everything together in one place.

Why Norton?

We're local. We know Norton. Unlike big platforms that try to cover everything everywhere, we focus exclusively on our town. This means better, more accurate listings, verified landlords from the community, and a platform that truly understands what Norton residents need.

How It Works

1

Free Listings for Landlords

Property owners can list their properties completely free. No hidden fees, no subscriptions.

2

Browse for Free

Tenants can browse all listings, view photos, and read descriptions without paying anything.

3

Small Unlock Fee

Only pay $2.50 when you're ready to contact a landlord. This keeps listings serious and reduces spam.

Our Values

Trust

We verify landlords and moderate listings to ensure authenticity.

Affordability

Low fees that work for everyone in our community.

Local Focus

Built specifically for Norton, by people who know Norton.
