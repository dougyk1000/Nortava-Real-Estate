General Questions

What is Nortava?
Nortava is a local-first real estate platform specifically built for Norton, Zimbabwe. We connect tenants and buyers with landlords and sellers, making property search and listing simple and affordable.
Is Nortava free to use?
Browsing listings is completely free. Landlords can list properties for free. Tenants only pay a small $2.50 fee when they want to unlock a landlord's contact details.
Why focus only on Norton?
By focusing on Norton, we can provide better, more accurate listings and truly understand the local market. We're not trying to be everything everywhere - we're trying to be the best option for our community.

For Tenants

How do I contact a landlord?
When you find a property you like, click "Unlock Contact" and pay the $2.50 fee using your preferred mobile payment method. You'll immediately get access to the landlord's phone number and can reach them via call or WhatsApp.
Is the unlock fee a one-time payment?
Yes! Once you pay to unlock a landlord's contact, you have permanent access. The contact details are saved in your dashboard forever.
What payment methods are accepted?
We accept EcoCash, OneMoney, Mukuru, and InnBucks - all popular mobile payment methods in Zimbabwe.

For Landlords

How do I list my property?
Create a free landlord account, go to your dashboard, and click "Add Listing". Fill in the property details and upload up to 3 photos. Your listing will be published immediately.
Do I pay anything to list?
No! Listing is completely free. There are no listing fees, monthly charges, or hidden costs. You only benefit when tenants unlock your contact.
How do I know when someone is interested?
You'll receive real-time notifications when a tenant unlocks your contact. You can also see all unlocks in your dashboard. Interested tenants will then reach out to you directly.

Safety & Security

Are landlords verified?
We have a verification process for landlords. Verified landlords display a badge on their listings. Always look for this badge and follow our safety tips when viewing properties.
How do I report a fake listing?
Use the "Report" button on any listing page, or visit our Report page. We take all reports seriously and investigate within 24 hours.
