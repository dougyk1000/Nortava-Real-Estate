Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 2024

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly:

  • Account information (name, email, phone number)
  • Property listing details (for landlords)
  • Payment information (processed by secure third-party providers)
  • Communications with our support team

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide and improve our services
  • Connect tenants with landlords
  • Process payments
  • Send important notifications about your account
  • Respond to support requests
  • Prevent fraud and ensure platform security

3. Information Sharing

We share information only in these cases:

  • With landlords when a tenant unlocks contact (contact details only)
  • With payment processors to complete transactions
  • When required by law
  • To protect our rights and prevent fraud

We never sell your personal information to third parties.

4. Data Storage

Your data is stored securely using Supabase, a trusted cloud database provider. We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate information
  • Delete your account and associated data
  • Opt out of marketing communications

6. Data Retention

We retain your data as long as your account is active. Upon account deletion, we remove your personal information within 30 days, except where required by law.

7. Security

We use encryption, secure connections (HTTPS), and regular security audits to protect your data. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy periodically. We will notify users of significant changes via email or platform notifications.

9. Contact Us

For privacy-related questions, please contact us.
