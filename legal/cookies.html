Cookie Policy

Last updated: December 2024

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help websites remember your preferences and provide a better user experience.

How We Use Cookies

Nortava uses cookies for:

  • Authentication: To keep you logged in across sessions
  • Preferences: To remember your theme choice (light/dark mode)
  • Session Management: To maintain your browsing session
  • Security: To help prevent fraud and protect your account

Types of Cookies We Use

Essential Cookies

Required for the platform to function. These cannot be disabled.

Preference Cookies

Store your preferences like theme settings.

Local Storage

We use local storage to save your session data and preferences locally on your device.

Third-Party Cookies

We do not use third-party tracking cookies or advertising cookies. We do not share your browsing data with advertisers.

Managing Cookies

You can manage cookies through your browser settings. Note that disabling essential cookies may affect your ability to use Nortava. Most browsers allow you to:

  • View and delete cookies
  • Block all or specific cookies
  • Set preferences for certain websites

Contact Us

If you have questions about our use of cookies, please contact us.
